Tanner Duve

I am a senior at the University of Pennsylvania studying computer science, logic, and philosophy. I am broadly interested in the logical foundations of mathematics and computation. In particular, I am interested in formal verification, functional programming, finite model theory, category theory, cryptography, and computational complexity. I have previously interned at Amazon and Meta, and have conducted research in programming languages, theorem proving, and databases. I am also a former Division I wide receiver for Penn's football team. Outside of comp sci I am passionate about veganism, fitness, and philosophy/theology.
